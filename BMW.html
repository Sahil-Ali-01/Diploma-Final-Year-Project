MODELS OF BMW

  • BMW G310R

    • The BMW G310 R is a standard or naked motorcycle developed by BMW Motorrad in collaboration with TVS Motor Company of India. It marks BMW's first modern sub-500 cc bike.
    It was shown in November 2015, and BMW said sales will begin in 2018.
    The bike is powered by a four-valve 313 cc (19.1 cu in) liquid-cooled single cylinder engine.
    BMW claims the top speed is 144 km/h (89 mph).BMW's claimed wet weight is 158.5 kg (349 lb).
