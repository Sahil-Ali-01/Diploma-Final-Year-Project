MODELS OF KAWASAKI

  • KAWASAKI Z900

    • The Kawasaki Z900 is a massive 20kg lighter than its predecessor.
    Powering the Japanese performance motorcycle is a 948cc in-line four-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine that is based on the Z1000’s motor.
    The engine churns out 125PS of power and 99Nm of peak torque and is mated to a 6-speed gearbox.
    Braking is done by disc brakes on both ends, while Nissin ABS is standard.
    The Kawasaki Z900 competes against the Ducati Monster 821 and the Triumph Street Triple.

  • KAWASAKI NINJA ZX-10R

    • Powering the motorcycle is the same 998cc, inline-four, liquid-cooled engine capable of producing 200PS of power at 13,000rpm and 113.5Nm of torque at 11,500rpm.
    It comes equipped with a twin spar cast aluminium frame, 43mm USD front forks and a rear monoshock.
    Braking is handled via Brembo 330mm dual disc brakes at the front and a single 220mm disc at the rear.
    It gets 17-inch wheels shod with 120/70 front and 190/55 rear tyres.
    The ZX-10R tips the scale at 206kg.

  • KAWASAKI NINJA 650

    • The 2019 Kawasaki Ninja 650 gets updated with updated styling, inspired from the supersport Kawasaki ZX-10R with sharper profile, twin headlamps and shortened tail section.
    The Ninja 650 is powered by a 649 cc parallel-twin engine which belts out 67 bhp of power and 65.7 Nm of torque.
    ABS is now offered as standard, and the new digital instrument console gets a gearshift indicator as well. Priced at Rs 5.49 lakh.
    Its maximum speed is 212 km/h.
