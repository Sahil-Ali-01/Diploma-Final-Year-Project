MODELS OF LAMBORGINI

  • LAMBORGINI AVENTADOR

    • The Lamborghini Aventador (Spanish pronunciation: [aβentaˈðoɾ]) is a mid-engine sports car produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini.
    In keeping with Lamborghini tradition, the Aventador is named after a fighting bull.
    Its top speed is 350 km/h(217 mph).





  • LAMBORGINI HURACAN

    • The Lamborghini Huracán (Spanish for "hurricane"; [uɾaˈkan]) is a sports car manufactured by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini replacing the previous V10 offering, the Gallardo.
    The Huracan made its worldwide debut at the 2014 Geneva Auto Show.
    Its maximum speed is 341 km/h(212 mph).



  • LAMBORGINI URUS

    • The Lamborghini Urus is an SUV manufactured by Italian automobile manufacturer Lamborghini.
    It was unveiled on 4 December 2017 and was put on the market for the 2018 model year.
    The name comes from the Urus, the ancestor of modern domestic cattle, also known as the aurochs.
    The Urus can accelerate from 0–100 km/h (62 mph) in 3.6 seconds and 0–200 km/h (124 mph) in 12.8 seconds.
